Red Panda

"Fire fox", a nickname for the red panda, is believed to be the inspiration for the name of Mozilla's flagship web browser.

  • Scientific Name: Ailurus fulgens
  • Average Length: 2 feet
  • Average Lifespan: 12-14 years
  • Habitat: Bamboo forests, temperate forests

Although it belongs to the order carnivora, the majority of the red panda's diet is comprised of bamboo. The name panda is believed to have originated from the word "ponya", meaning bamboo or plant-eating animal in Nepali.
